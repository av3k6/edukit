Quality School Supply Kits

Customized for Your District

did you know?

Since 1993, we’ve packed and shipped more than 34 million items in our kits and put school supplies in the hands of more than 1 million happy students.

At EduKit, we believe that “one-to-one” shouldn’t just apply to digital equity, but to hands-on equity. Our seamless, easy, and affordable school supply kit program puts a complete kit in the hands of every student, giving each child equal access to the quality supplies they need —from easy-grip scissors to graphing calculators.

Our kits are fully customizable to your needs across every grade from K through 12. We’ll work within your budget, and you can even apply ESSER I, II & Title I funding toward kit purchasing. Save time and money for your district, your teachers, and your community. Our expert team is ready to show you how easy it can be!

YOUR PROGRAM

Kits are customized and packaged according to your district’s needs, and can be shipped to your district warehouse, individual schools, or students' homes to accommodate remote learners. Want your kits packaged in backpacks? No problem.

EduKit has a solution for every budget. You can choose between name brand products or quality brand equivalents to meet your needs. By purchasing kits in lieu of bulk items you will save many hours of district personnel's time and eliminate the hassle of individual item distribution.
